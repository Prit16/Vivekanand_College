College Campus

The college is situated in an area that emanates tranquility and free of the humdrum of city life. The college is positioned beside saroli bridge ,jahangirpura,Surat.




Value based activities

The college believes in imbibing ethical and cultural values in students along with academic excellence. Towards this purpose the college organizes various activities that are part and parcel of our Indian way of life. Apart from celebrating various festivals in a traditional ways college also organizing programs like palkhi yatra,satyanarayan katha pujan, environment awareness rally and activities based on swami vivekanandji's teachings.
