about Vivekanand

Vivekanand college came into being in 2001 with the mission of providing education in various disciplines like computers, commerce, education and management. It is situated near Saroli Bridge, Jahangirpura, away from the hustle and bustle of the city, thereby providing a peaceful and ideal environment for education, Vivekanand college is affiliated to Veer Narmad South Gujarat University and is managed by Abhinav Education Trust. The trust believe in multi-dimensional development of students which will enable them to serve the nation with their versatile qualities.
